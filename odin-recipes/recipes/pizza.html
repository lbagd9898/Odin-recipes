Pizza

This recipe yields a fabulous pizza with pepperoni.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • pepperoni
    •

    Steps

    1. Roll out the dough into a large circle shape.
    2. Spread the sauce onto the dough.
    3. Sprinkle the cheese onto the sauce.
    4. Place the pepperoni onto the cheese.
